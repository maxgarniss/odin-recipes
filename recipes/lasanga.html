Deliciously Layered: Ultimate Homemade Lasagna Recipe for Pasta Perfection!

Indulge in the comforting embrace of our Ultimate Homemade Lasagna, where layers of rich marinara sauce, creamy béchamel, and perfectly seasoned ground beef mingle with tender lasagna noodles for a culinary symphony of flavors. This beloved classic is a labor of love, starting with the careful crafting of a robust marinara sauce simmered to perfection, ensuring each bite bursts with vibrant tomato goodness. Assembling this masterpiece is an art form, where tender lasagna noodles are lovingly layered with generous dollops of creamy béchamel sauce, creating a luscious, melt-in-your-mouth texture. The crowning glory? A golden blanket of bubbling mozzarella and Parmesan cheese, perfectly melded to create a crispy, irresistible topping. Whether served as a comforting family dinner or showcased at a festive gathering, this Ultimate Homemade Lasagna is sure to steal the show and leave everyone craving seconds.

  • 1 pound (450g) ground beef
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 24 ounces (680g) marinara sauce
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 12 lasagna noodles
  • 4 cups (950ml) béchamel sauce
  • 2 cups (200g) shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1 cup (100g) grated parmesan cheese
  • Fresh basil leaves for garnish (optional)

  1. Prepare the Meat Sauce:

    In a large skillet, heat olive oil over medium heat. Add chopped onions and minced garlic, sauté until softened and fragrant.

  2. Cook the Lasagna Noodles:

    Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil. Add the lasagna noodles and cook according to the package instructions until al dente.

    Once cooked, drain the noodles and rinse them under cold water to stop the cooking process. Set aside.

  3. Preheat the Oven:

    Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).

  4. Assemble the Lasagna:

    Spread a thin layer of the meat sauce on the bottom of a 9x13 inch baking dish.

    Place a layer of lasagna noodles over the sauce, slightly overlapping them.

    Spread a generous layer of béchamel sauce over the noodles, followed by a layer of the meat sauce.

    Sprinkle a portion of shredded mozzarella and grated Parmesan cheese over the meat sauce.

    Repeat the layers until all ingredients are used, finishing with a layer of cheese on top.

  5. Bake the Lasagna:

    Cover the baking dish with aluminum foil and bake in the preheated oven for 25-30 minutes.

    Remove the foil and continue baking for an additional 10-15 minutes, or until the cheese is melted and bubbly, and the edges are golden brown.

  6. Serve:

    Allow the lasagna to cool for a few minutes before slicing.

    Garnish with fresh basil leaves, if desired, and serve hot.

Enjoy the rich and comforting flavors of this Ultimate Homemade Lasagna with your family and friends!
